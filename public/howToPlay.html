How to Play?

Requirments Play Against Yourself Game Setup(New) Game Setup(Join) Game Play Game End
  • Google Chrome Browser
  • Metamask Extention, install it here
  • Set your network in metamask to Ropsten
  • If you don't have any ether in your account, get some for free here or here
  1. Since this is only a protfolio project, chances are that you are here to checkout the developer talent and not actually bet your ETH.
  2. Simply open this in another tab, you can click here.
  3. Play as player 1 in one tab by creating a new game.
  4. Play as player 2 in the other tab by joining the game.
  1. Click on button 'Connect To Metamask'
  2. In the metamask popup, click 'Connect'
  3. Click 'New Game'. Enter the amount you want to bet in ETH. Click 'Submit'
  4. Wait till you transaction is minned. This can take around 30 seconds.
  5. Game ID will appear on the screen after minning is done. Share this Game ID with your opponent
  6. Game will Start automatically after your opponent join's
  1. Click on button 'Connect To Metamask'
  2. In the metamask popup, click 'Connect'
  3. Click 'Join Game'. Enter the Game ID shared by your opponent. Click 'Submit'
  4. Wait till you transaction is minned. This can take around 30 seconds.
  5. Game will Start automatically after the transaction is minned.
  1. The message below the board will tell you if it is your turn or not.
  2. To make you move, click any box inside the board.
  3. In the metamask popup, click 'Sign'
  4. Game will end automatically if there is a winner or draw.
  • If the game ended in draw, then you will be refunded the amount you bet.
  • If you won, you will be credited the twice the amount you bet
  • Nothing will be credited if you lost.
  • Please note, only in the case anyone wins, 10% of the total bet will be charged as commission.
